About The Future was Flash and IPX Forever

TFWF and IPXF were started as personal project mostly to create an SWF archive for myself. It has been a lot of learning as I go and while it has been frustrating at times, I think it has turned out well, or at least good enough for me. I decided after the site was stable enough to make it public. That said, I hope you enjoy using it as much as I enjoyed enjoy making it.

The Road Ahead

I intend to keep adding games and improving the site as long as I can. As always you can suggest new games to be added.
